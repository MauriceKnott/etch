Commercial Floor Cleaning Services

We believe that Commercial floor care is an important part of creating a clean and healthy environment for customers, clients and employees. This is why we offer commercial floor cleaning services which specialize primarily in large area commercial carpet cleaning as well as hard surface tile and grout cleaning. Our carpet cleaners utilize hot water extraction technology and environmentally friendly solutions when cleaning commercial carpets in order to minimize environmental impacts. Stains are pre-treated to provide the best possible stain removal. We also specialize in tile and grout cleaning, hardwood floor cleaning and stone, marble and granite cleaning and restoration. Our specialized industrial spin-ray machine technology uses a combination of pressurized water and cleaning solution, spinning brushes and vacuum suction to clean or polish hard surfaces. Abrasive materials can be added to smooth and polish many different types of stone and flooring surfaces. Call us to schedule an evaluation of your flooring and allow us create a cleaning or polishing plan that meets your needs.
